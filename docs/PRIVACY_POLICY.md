## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the LLM Search Behavior Tracker ("the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you participate in our research study.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Data

When you use the Extension, we automatically collect:

1. **Browsing Activity**
   - URLs of pages you visit
   - Page titles
   - Time spent on each page
   - Navigation patterns (page visits, back/forward navigation)

2. **User Interactions**
   - Mouse clicks (location and target element)
   - Scroll events (position and depth)
   - Form interactions (field types, not values)
   - Search queries entered into search engines and LLM interfaces

3. **Page Structure**
   - DOM snapshots (page structure, not full content)
   - Links and images on pages
   - Form fields present (types only, not values)

4. **Technical Information**
   - Browser user agent
   - Screen resolution
   - Viewport size
   - Timezone
   - Tab management (switches, focus events)

### User-Provided Data

You provide:
- Participant ID
- Consent confirmation
- Optional survey responses

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

- Passwords or authentication credentials
- Credit card numbers or payment information
- Social Security numbers or tax IDs
- Private messages or emails (content)
- Health records or medical information
- Precise geolocation data
- Data from incognito/private browsing windows
- Data from excluded domains (configurable by you)

## How We Use Your Information

### Research Purposes Only

Your data is used solely for academic research to:
- Understand search behavior differences between LLMs and traditional search engines
- Analyze decision-making processes during information search
- Study user experience with different search tools
- Generate insights for improving search technologies

### Specific Uses

1. **Data Analysis**: Statistical analysis of browsing and search patterns
2. **Academic Publications**: Anonymized, aggregated findings published in journals/conferences
3. **Data Sharing**: Anonymized datasets may be shared with other researchers
4. **Education**: Data may be used for teaching purposes (fully anonymized)

### What We Do NOT Do

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for commercial advertising
- Share identifiable data with anyone outside the research team
- Use data for purposes other than this research study

### Security Measures

### Data Retention

## Data Anonymization

### Participant Anonymity

Your data is anonymized through:
- Use of participant ID instead of personal information
- No collection of names, emails, or IP addresses
- No linking to other personal accounts or profiles
- Removal of any accidental personally identifiable information

### Published Data

When data is published or shared:
- Only aggregate statistics are reported
- Individual participants are not identifiable
- Data is reviewed to remove any identifying information

## Your Privacy Controls

### Extension Controls

You can:
- **Pause Recording**: Temporarily stop data collection
- **Exclude Domains**: Prevent tracking on specific websites
- **Review Settings**: View what's being tracked
- **Stop Participation**: Uninstall extension at any time

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your collected data

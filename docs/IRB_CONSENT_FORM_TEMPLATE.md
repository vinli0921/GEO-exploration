## Introduction

You are being invited to participate in a research study. Before you decide whether to participate, it is important for you to understand why the research is being done and what it will involve. Please take the time to read the following information carefully.

## Purpose of the Study

The purpose of this research is to understand how people search for information using Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, compared to traditional search engines like Google. This research will help us understand:

- How search behaviors differ between LLMs and traditional search engines
- What types of queries people use with different search tools
- How people navigate and evaluate information from different sources
- Decision-making processes during online search tasks

## What Will Participation Involve?

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to:

1. **Install a Chrome Extension**: Install a research tracking extension in your Chrome browser
2. **Provide Consent**: Review and agree to this consent form
3. **Enter Participant ID**: Use the unique participant ID provided to you
4. **Browse Normally**: Continue your normal browsing activities for [INSERT DURATION]
5. **Complete Survey** (Optional): Fill out a brief survey about your experience

**Time Commitment**: The extension will track your browsing for [INSERT DURATION, e.g., "1-2 hours" or "up to 1 week"]

## What Data Will Be Collected?

The Chrome extension will collect the following data while you browse:

### Automatically Collected:
- **Page visits**: URLs and titles of pages you visit
- **User interactions**: Clicks, scrolls, and navigation patterns
- **Search queries**: Text you enter into search engines and LLM interfaces
- **Dwell time**: How long you spend on each page
- **Page structure**: Basic DOM snapshots of page layouts
- **Browser metadata**: Browser type, screen size, timezone

### NOT Collected:
- Passwords or authentication credentials
- Credit card or financial information
- Social Security numbers or other sensitive IDs
- Content of private messages or emails
- Data from incognito/private browsing windows

## Data Privacy and Security

### Anonymization
- Your data will be identified only by your participant ID
- No personally identifiable information (name, email, IP address) will be linked to your data
- Data is transmitted over encrypted connections

### Data Storage
- Data is stored on secure, password-protected servers
- Only authorized research team members have access
- Data will be retained for [INSERT RETENTION PERIOD, e.g., "5 years"] as required by IRB regulations

### Data Use
- Data will be used only for this research study

### Excluded Websites
You can configure the extension to exclude specific websites (e.g., banking, healthcare sites) from tracking.

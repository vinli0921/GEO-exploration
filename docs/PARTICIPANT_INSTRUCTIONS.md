## Getting Started

### Step 1: Open the Extension

Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar

### Step 2: Read the Consent Form

1. Review the informed consent carefully
2. Read about:
   - What data is collected
   - How your privacy is protected
   - Your rights as a participant
3. Ask questions if anything is unclear

### Step 3: Enter Your Participant ID

Enter the participant ID given to you by the researcher:
- Example: `P001`, `P042`, `STUDY-123`
- This ID is used to identify your data anonymously

### Step 4: Provide Consent

1. Check the box: "I have read and agree to participate"
2. Click "Start Recording"

### Step 5: Browse Normally

That's it! Continue browsing as you normally would:
- The extension runs in the background
- A green dot indicates recording is active
- Your data is automatically saved

### What to Do

- **Browse normally**: Use Chrome as you usually would
- **Complete your tasks**: Follow any specific tasks given by researchers
- **Keep extension running**: Don't disable or remove it during the study period

### What's Being Tracked

- ‚ùå Passwords or login credentials

- ‚ùå Private messages or emails

### Privacy Features

#### Exclude Sensitive Sites

You can exclude specific websites from tracking:

1. Click the extension icon
2. Click "Advanced Settings"
3. Under "Exclude Domains", enter domains (one per line):
   ```
   mybank.com
   healthcare-portal.org
   private-email.com
   ```
4. Click "Save Settings"

These sites will not be tracked at all.

#### Pause Recording

If you need to temporarily pause recording:
1. Click the extension icon
2. Click "Pause" button
3. Resume when ready

**Note**: Try to avoid pausing unless necessary, as continuous data is most valuable for the study.

## Troubleshooting

### Extension Won't Install

**Problem**: Error message when loading extension

**Solutions**:
- Try restarting Chrome
- Contact the research team

### Extension Icon Not Visible

**Problem**: Can't find the extension icon

**Solutions**:
- Click the puzzle piece (üß©) in Chrome toolbar
- Find the extension and click the pin icon
- Check that extension is enabled at `chrome://extensions/`

### Recording Won't Start

**Problem**: "Start Recording" button doesn't work

**Solutions**:
- Make sure you entered a participant ID
- Make sure you checked the consent box
- Try closing and reopening the extension popup
- Restart Chrome if needed

### Data Not Uploading

**Problem**: Error message about upload failure

**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection
- The extension will retry automatically
- Data is saved locally and won't be lost
- Contact research team if problem persists

### Performance Issues

**Problem**: Browser feels slow with extension running

**Solutions**:
- The extension is lightweight, but complex websites may be affected slightly
- Close unused tabs to improve performance
- Contact research team if issues persist - they may adjust settings

### Excluded Sites Still Being Tracked

**Problem**: Excluded domain still appears to be tracked

**Solutions**:
- Make sure you entered just the domain (e.g., `example.com` not `https://www.example.com`)
- Click "Save Settings" after adding domains
- Close and reopen extension popup to confirm settings saved
- Restart recording if needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use other browsers?

No, this extension only works in Chrome. Please use Chrome for all browsing during the study period.

### Can I use multiple computers?

Yes, but install the extension separately on each computer with your same participant ID.

### What if I forget to start recording?

Contact the research team. They may be able to extend your study period.

### Can I see my data?

Yes! Contact the research team with your participant ID to request a copy of your data.

### Can I delete my data?

Yes, you can request deletion by contacting the research team. This must be done before data analysis begins (researcher will inform you of the deadline).

### Will this affect my Chrome browsing?

Minimally. The extension is designed to be lightweight and should not noticeably affect your browsing experience.

### Can other people see my data?

No. Your data is identified only by your participant ID and is stored securely. Only the research team has access.

### What if I visit an embarrassing website?

You can:
1. Add it to excluded domains
2. Use incognito mode (not tracked)
3. Pause recording temporarily

Remember, your data is anonymous and the research team views data in aggregate.

### What if I accidentally enter sensitive information?

Don't worry. The extension is designed to:
- Redact most input fields
- Only capture search queries (not passwords or payment info)
- Skip sensitive form fields

If you're concerned, contact the research team.
